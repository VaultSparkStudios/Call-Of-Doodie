# Content Plan

## Content pillars

- Enemies: readable, funny, escalating, and mechanically distinct
- Weapons and perks: synergy-rich and replayable
- Bosses: memorable, high-pressure, and comedy-forward
- Micro-writing: death lines, labels, and feedback should reinforce the brand voice

## Ongoing expansion targets

- More meaningful perk synergies rather than raw count inflation
- Boss polish and boss telegraph clarity
- Daily challenge variety through seed logic and modifier design
- Social challenge content that feels taunting and shareable without toxic tone

## Workflow

- Create content ideas with humor-first filter
- Validate gameplay readability before shipping jokes
- Keep balance notes with each new enemy, perk, or boss addition
